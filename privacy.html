Privacy Policy

Last updated: Oct 8, 2025

Blossom & Blade (“we,” “us,” “our”), operated by Venus Venue LLC, provides an adult-only, AI-only chat experience. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Plainly: we keep things private, log the minimum we need for safety and operations, and we do not sell your personal information.

1) What we collect

  • Account & purchase info: details provided at checkout (handled by our payment processor), purchase history, plan type (Day Pass / Monthly).
  • Chat content: messages you send and AI replies. For Monthly plans, we may store history (“memory”) while your plan is active so conversations feel continuous.
  • Safety & operations: limited technical logs (IP, device info, timestamps) for security, fraud prevention, abuse mitigation, and reliability.
  • Consent flags (local device): one-time gate confirmations in your browser’s localStorage (bb18, bbTerms, bbRules, bbRefund, and bbConsentAt) to avoid re-prompting.
  • Cookies/local storage: used for session, preferences, and the 18+ gate. We don’t use third-party ad trackers.

2) How we use your information

  • Provide the service (AI chat), personalize character responses, and maintain features you enable (e.g., conversation memory on Monthly).
  • Process payments and prevent fraud (via our payment processor).
  • Operate, secure, and improve the site, including troubleshooting and aggregate/de-identified analytics.
  • Comply with law, handle disputes, and enforce Terms (including refunds within 24 hours for verified technical access failures).

3) Safety, moderation, and limited logging

  • Crisis support pop-up: if self-harm risk phrases are detected, we may show a resource pop-up (e.g., U.S. 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). The AI aims to reassure and redirect to help.
  • Internal alert: clear self-harm intent or severe risk signals may trigger an internal alert to our safety contact.
  • Minimal safety log: for investigating violations, safety, fraud, or disputes, we may temporarily retain limited excerpts (e.g., the last five (5) messages), timestamps, and a session identifier. These are used only for safety/ops and are not shared for marketing.
  • Policy enforcement: repeated or severe violations (e.g., minors, bestiality, non-consensual harm, trafficking, DV-play, scat, necrophilia, threats, illegal activity) may result in suspension or termination.

4) Sharing

  • Payment processors: to take payments and handle disputes (they receive necessary billing info).
  • Hosting & infrastructure: to run the site and store data securely.
  • Analytics/monitoring: to keep the service reliable (aggregate or de-identified where possible).
  • Legal & safety: to comply with law, respond to legal requests, prevent fraud, and protect users and our service.

We do not sell your personal information.

5) Storage & retention

  • Conversation memory (Monthly plans): kept while your plan is active; may be reset if your plan lapses.
  • Day Pass: we keep only what’s needed to provide access and operate the service; long-term memory is not retained after access ends.
  • Safety excerpts: kept only as long as needed for investigation and dispute handling, then minimized or deleted.
  • Operational logs: kept for a limited time to secure and operate the service, then rotated or deleted.

6) Your choices

  • One-time gate: clear site data in your browser to remove local consent flags; you’ll be re-prompted next visit.
  • Access/correction/deletion: email us to request a copy, correction, or deletion of account-linked data, subject to verification and legal exceptions.
  • Communications: opt out of non-essential emails; transactional emails (like receipts) may still be sent.

7) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No system is perfectly secure. Keep your device and access credentials safe.

8) Children

This service is for adults 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the service, contact us and we will act promptly.

9) International users

Data may be processed in the United States and other locations where we or our providers operate. By using the service, you understand your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in these locations.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The effective date appears at the top. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
